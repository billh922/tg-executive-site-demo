30 · 60 · 90 Day Value Plan

A structured approach to delivering measurable impact from the first week of a fractional engagement — balancing rapid discovery with disciplined execution across customer retention, team performance, and revenue health. Every engagement is different; this framework adapts to what matters most to your business right now.

Day 30
LISTEN · LEARN · MAP
Foundation & Discovery
  • Conduct 1-on-1s with every CS team member and key cross-functional stakeholders (Sales, Product, Support, Finance)
  • Review existing customer health data, churn history, and NRR trends for the last 4 quarters
  • Audit current CS tooling, playbooks, and coverage model against ARR and headcount
  • Map the 20 highest-risk accounts and identify the top 3 intervention opportunities
  • Establish weekly operating cadence and reporting structure with leadership
Deliverable

A written current-state assessment: where revenue is at risk, what the CS team is missing, and the top 3 highest-leverage changes to make in the next 60 days. Presented to the CEO or CRO in a 60-minute working session.

Day 60
PRIORITIZE · ALIGN · QUICK WINS
Strategic Alignment & Early Wins
  • Implement health scoring framework and risk tiering across the full customer portfolio
  • Launch high-touch intervention program for the 20 identified at-risk accounts
  • Redesign QBR process for strategic accounts — executive-facing, outcome-oriented
  • Introduce CSM performance metrics aligned to NRR, not activity volume
  • Close or advance at least 2 at-risk renewals with documented save strategy
Deliverable

First measurable wins on the board: at-risk accounts stabilized, health scoring live, QBR redesign in motion. Updated risk report showing movement since Day 30 baseline. CS team has a shared operating language and understands what success looks like.

Day 90
EXECUTE · BUILD · GOVERN
Retention Engine in Motion
  • CS playbook v1 complete and in use — onboarding, EBR, renewal, and expansion motions documented
  • Pipeline of expansion opportunities identified and handed to Sales with CS context
  • CS hiring plan finalized if headcount gaps identified in Day 30 assessment
  • Tooling gaps addressed — Gainsight configuration, Salesforce CS views, or equivalent
  • Monthly CS business review format established for ongoing leadership reporting
Deliverable

A CS organization that operates with shared standards, clear metrics, and a documented playbook. NRR trend line moving in the right direction. The team can run the system without daily dependence on the fractional engagement — which is the point.

What Patrice Is Looking For

Series B through late-stage SaaS companies where post-sale is the next lever for growth. Revenue leaders who want an operator who builds infrastructure that lasts — not a deck.
