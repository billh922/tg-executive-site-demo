Executive Summary

Patrice Cappello is a go-to-market and industry strategy executive with 25+ years building revenue in enterprise SaaS. She spent 9 years as Managing Director at Workday leading GTM strategy for the Professional and Business Services vertical — one of the most complex and relationship-driven segments in enterprise software. Before Workday, she was North America Industry Lead for Professional Services at SAP, and held VP Sales and Sales Engineering leadership roles at Deltek. She has launched Cappello Strategy Group to bring that depth of expertise to SaaS companies on a fractional basis.
