Executive Summary

Patrice Cappello has spent 18 years doing one thing exceptionally well: turning customer relationships into durable revenue. From Oracle to ServiceNow to Salesforce to Workday, she has built CS organizations from scratch, scaled them through hypergrowth, and redesigned them when the market shifted. Her track record spans $2B+ in managed ARR and hundreds of enterprise accounts across SaaS, HCM, and cloud infrastructure.

What distinguishes her approach is the refusal to treat customer success as a support function. In every organization she has led, CS was repositioned as a revenue protection and expansion engine — with the metrics, the executive access, and the seat at the table to prove it. She brings operational rigor to a function that too often runs on relationships alone.

Most recently as VP Customer Success at Workday, Patrice led a global team of 300+ and delivered 97% net retention in the enterprise segment — consistently above the SaaS industry median. She is now available for fractional VP CS engagements with Series B through late-stage SaaS companies that are ready to professionalize their post-sale motion.

Core Expertise

CS Organization Design
Built CS functions from zero to scale at three enterprise SaaS companies. Designed coverage models, hiring frameworks, and career ladders that reduced attrition and improved CSM-to-ARR ratios. At Workday, restructured a 200-person org to align by segment, cutting time-to-value by 40%.
Net Revenue Retention
Oversaw $2B+ in ARR with a relentless focus on NRR as the primary health metric. Built health scoring systems, churn prediction models, and expansion playbooks that consistently delivered net retention above 95% in enterprise segments.
Executive Stakeholder Management
Skilled at navigating C-suite relationships on both sides of the table — managing internal executive sponsors and serving as the customer's strategic advocate at the board level. Designed and ran QBR programs that converted renewals into multi-year expansions.
CS Technology & Tooling
Deep expertise in Gainsight, Salesforce, Totango, and custom CS platforms. Led multiple tool migrations and consolidations, including a full Gainsight implementation at Workday that unified health data across seven product lines.
Onboarding & Time-to-Value
Designed onboarding programs that cut average time-to-value by 35% at ServiceNow and reduced first-90-day churn risk significantly. Believes onboarding is where retention is won or lost — and operationalizes accordingly.
Fractional CS Leadership
Available for fractional VP CS engagements where the work is to build, repair, or professionalize the post-sale function. Specializes in the critical window between $10M and $100M ARR where CS infrastructure either scales or breaks.
